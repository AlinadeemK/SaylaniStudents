Saylani Mass IT Training Program Offering Free Of Cost Latest IT Training

Saylani Mass Training Program is a vocational training program run by the Saylani Welfare International Trust, a non-profit organization based in Karachi, Pakistan. The program offers free vocational training in various fields, such as IT (Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Power BI, Deep Learning, Machine Learning), sewing and tailoring, culinary arts, beautician courses, and more. The program aims to provide skills-based education and training to underprivileged individuals in Pakistan who may not have had access to formal education or vocational training. By equipping these individuals with practical skills, Saylani Mass Training Program helps them become self-sufficient and earn a livelihood for themselves and their families. The Saylani Mass Training Program has trained thousands of individuals since its inception in 2015, and it continues to expand its reach by opening new training centers across Pakistan. The program has received widespread recognition and support from both the public and private sectors in Pakistan for its efforts to address poverty and unemployment through vocational training.
